Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist:-

Do's

1. Think before you post - always assume that anything you share online can stay there permanently.

2.Use respectful and professional language in all your interactions.

3. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading false content.

4. Manage your privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal details.

5. Share meaningful content like your achievements, projects, and skills to build a positive online image.

Don'ts

1. Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or private details.

2. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content that could harm others or your reputation.

3. Never spread rumors or misinformation without checking facts.

4. Avoid unnecessary arguments or online conflicts, as they can create a negative impression.

5. Don't post when you are emotional or angry, as it may lead to regret later.
